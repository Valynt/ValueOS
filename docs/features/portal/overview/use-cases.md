## 🎯 Common Patterns

Across all industries, successful ValueOS implementations share these patterns:

### Start Small
Begin with a single team or product area. Demonstrate value before expanding.

### Connect to Existing Metrics
Map engineering work to metrics your business already tracks. Don't create new metrics.

### Involve Leadership Early
Get executive buy-in by showing immediate visibility into engineering impact.

### Iterate on Value Definitions
Your definition of "value" will evolve. ValueOS adapts as your understanding deepens.
