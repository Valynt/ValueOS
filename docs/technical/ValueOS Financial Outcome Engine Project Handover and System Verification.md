> **"Generic LLMs gave the world the speed of thought; ValueOS gives the enterprise the weight of truth."**

### VMRT Structural Integrity
Every financial claim within ValueOS is supported by a hashable, auditable chain of logic:
1.  **Causal Mapping:** Linking operational friction (e.g., 8-day monthly close) to financial drivers (EBITDA erosion).

3.  **Deterministic Math:** Utilizing formula interpreters rather than token prediction for multi-step arithmetic.
