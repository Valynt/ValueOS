# Data Ownership Statement

## Ownership Principles

- Customers retain ownership of their Customer Data submitted to ValueOS.
- ValueOS acts as a service provider/processor for Customer Data under applicable agreements.
- ValueOS does not sell Customer Data.

## Customer Rights

Subject to contract and applicable law, customers may:

- Access and export their data.
- Request correction or deletion workflows where supported.
- Define retention or deletion requirements consistent with service capabilities.

## ValueOS Responsibilities

ValueOS is responsible for:

- Processing Customer Data only to provide, secure, and support the contracted services.
- Implementing administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards.
- Limiting internal access to authorized personnel with business need.

## Data Use Boundaries

- Service telemetry and operational metadata may be used for reliability, security, billing, and abuse prevention.
- Any product-improvement use of customer content follows contractual commitments and configurable product controls.
- Data sharing with subprocessors is limited to what is necessary to deliver services.

## Data Return and Deletion

At contract termination or on valid request (as applicable), ValueOS supports data return and deletion workflows according to contractual terms, legal obligations, and backup lifecycle constraints.

For legal terms precedence, refer to the signed customer agreement and applicable data processing addendum.
