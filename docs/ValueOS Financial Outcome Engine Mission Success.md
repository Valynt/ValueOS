6.  **IntegrityAgent:** The "Financial Firewall" enforcing the **ValueOS Manifesto**.

The Value Fabric serves as the immutable nervous system of the enterprise. It utilizes **Value Modeling Reasoning Traces (VMRT)** to ensure every financial claim is auditable. By anchoring these traces in the **Economic Structure Ontology (ESO)**, ValueOS ensures that all calculations are governed by industry-standard benchmarks (APQC, BLS, NRF) rather than AI hallucinations.
