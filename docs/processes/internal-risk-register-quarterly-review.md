# Internal Risk Register Template & Quarterly Review Process

**Owner:** Compliance + Security  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Reviewed:** 2026-02-13  
**Cadence:** Quarterly

---

## Purpose

Define a consistent internal risk register template and a quarterly review process tied to incident outcomes and compliance evidence.

---

## Risk Register Template

Use one record per discrete risk.

| Risk ID | Domain | Risk Statement | Inherent Likelihood | Inherent Impact | Existing Controls | Residual Likelihood | Residual Impact | Risk Rating | Owner | Treatment Plan | Target Date | Status | Linked Incidents | Linked Compliance Findings | Last Reviewed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| R-YYYY-001 | Security | Example risk statement | Medium | High | MFA + monitoring | Low | Medium | Medium | Team Owner | Control enhancement | 2026-05-30 | Open | INC-001 | COMP-003 | 2026-02-13 |

### Field Guidance

- **Domain:** Security, Privacy, Reliability, Legal, Operational.
- **Risk Statement:** "If [condition], then [impact], leading to [business consequence]."
- **Risk Rating:** determined from residual likelihood x residual impact matrix.
- **Linked Incidents:** IDs from incident management workflow.
- **Linked Compliance Findings:** findings from audits, control tests, vendor reviews.

---

## Quarterly Review Process

1. **Collect Inputs (Week 1)**
   - Prior quarter incidents and postmortems.
   - Compliance testing results and audit findings.
   - Open remediation actions from vendor/security programs.
2. **Refresh Register (Week 2)**
   - Add new risks discovered from incidents/findings.
   - Re-score existing risks based on latest control state.
   - Retire risks with verified closure evidence.
3. **Review Meeting (Week 3)**
   - Validate top risks and risk trend changes.
   - Confirm treatment plans, owners, and deadlines.
   - Escalate risks above tolerance threshold.
4. **Publish Outputs (Week 4)**
   - Finalized quarterly risk register snapshot.
   - Top risk summary and mitigation commitments.
   - Compliance and incident linkage evidence archive.

---

## Required Artifacts Per Quarter

- Updated risk register export.
- Meeting notes with attendance and approvals.
- Risk treatment action log with status changes.
- Mapping sheet linking risks to incidents and compliance findings.

---

## Escalation and Governance

- High residual risks without approved treatment plan are escalated to leadership.
- Repeated overdue actions are reviewed in compliance governance meeting.
- Risk trends are incorporated into annual planning and control roadmap.
