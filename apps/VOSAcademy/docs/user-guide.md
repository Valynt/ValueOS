# VOS Academy User Guide

## Welcome to VOS Academy

VOS Academy is your comprehensive platform for mastering Value Operating System (VOS) methodologies. This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your learning experience.

## Getting Started

### Account Setup
1. **Sign Up**: Create your account with your email address
2. **Profile Completion**: Add your role (Sales, Customer Success, Marketing, etc.) and experience level
3. **Onboarding Tour**: Complete the guided tour to familiarize yourself with the platform

### Navigation
- **Dashboard**: Your personalized learning hub
- **Pillars**: Access the 10 VOS methodology pillars
- **Simulations**: Practice with interactive scenarios
- **AI Tutor**: Get personalized guidance and support
- **Analytics**: Track your progress and performance

## Learning Path

### The 10 VOS Pillars
VOS Academy follows a structured learning path through 10 foundational pillars:

1. **Unified Value Language** - Establish common terminology
2. **Value Data Model** - Structure value quantification
3. **Discovery Excellence** - Identify value opportunities
4. **Business Case Development** - Build compelling justifications
5. **Value Realization** - Execute and measure outcomes
6. **Value Engineering** - Optimize value delivery
7. **Value Communication** - Share insights effectively
8. **Value Culture** - Build organizational capabilities
9. **Value Leadership** - Drive strategic initiatives
10. **Value Innovation** - Create breakthrough solutions

### Recommended Learning Sequence
- Start with Pillars 1-4 (Foundation)
- Complete interactive exercises for each pillar
- Take assessments to validate understanding
- Practice with simulations before certification

## Interactive Features

### Simulations
Simulations provide hands-on practice with real-world scenarios:

- **Business Case Development**: Create ROI justifications
- **QBR Expansion Modeling**: Plan account growth strategies
- **AI-Powered Evaluation**: Receive instant feedback on your approach
- **40/30/30 Scoring**: Balanced assessment across technical, cross-functional, and AI-augmented skills

### AI Tutor
Your personal VOS learning assistant offers:

- **ROI Narrative Generation**: Craft compelling business stories
- **Value Case Building**: Develop comprehensive business cases
- **KPI Hypothesis Validation**: Test and refine metrics
- **Real-time Guidance**: Get help with specific VOS concepts

### Certifications
Demonstrate your expertise with tiered certifications:

- **Bronze**: 80%+ overall score - Solid understanding
- **Silver**: 85%+ overall score - Strong application skills
- **Gold**: 95%+ overall score - Expert-level mastery

## Assessment System

### Quiz Structure
- Multiple choice and scenario-based questions
- Adaptive difficulty based on performance
- Immediate feedback and explanations

### Scoring Methodology
Final certification scores use a 40/30/30 rubric:
- **40% Quiz Performance**: Knowledge assessment
- **30% Simulation Performance**: Application skills
- **30% Role Task Engagement**: Practical engagement metrics

## Progress Tracking

- Knowledge gain metrics

### Personalized Recommendations
- Next recommended learning activities

## Best Practices

### Effective Learning
1. **Complete exercises in order** - Each pillar builds on previous knowledge
2. **Practice regularly** - Use simulations to reinforce concepts
3. **Apply to real work** - Use VOS methodologies in your daily activities
4. **Seek feedback** - Leverage the AI tutor for guidance

### Certification Preparation
- Review all pillar content before assessments
- Practice with multiple simulation scenarios
- Focus on understanding over memorization
- Use AI tutor for clarification on complex topics
