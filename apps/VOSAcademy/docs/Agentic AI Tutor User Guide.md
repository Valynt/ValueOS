# Agentic AI Tutor User Guide

## Overview

The **VOS Agentic AI Tutor** is your intelligent learning companion designed to help you master the Value Operating System through personalized, context-aware guidance. Unlike traditional chatbots, this AI agent actively helps you **compose**, **build**, and **practice** value-based methodologies in real-time.

The AI Tutor adapts to your role (Sales, Customer Success, Marketing, Product, Executive Leadership, or Value Engineering) and current maturity level (L0-L5) to provide tailored support.

---

## Accessing the AI Tutor

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → Click **"AI Tutor"** in the navigation menu
2. Or visit directly: `/ai-tutor` from any page

The AI Tutor interface displays:
- Your current **role** and **maturity level** badges (top right)
- Four specialized **mode tabs** (Chat, KPI, ROI, Value Case)
- Conversation history with markdown formatting
- Quick action buttons for common tasks

---

## Four Specialized Modes

### 1. 💬 Chat Mode

**Purpose:** General VOS guidance, concept clarification, and methodology questions

**Best For:**
- Understanding VOS frameworks and principles
- Getting quick answers about value-based selling
- Clarifying terminology and best practices
- Exploring how to apply VOS concepts to your role

**Example Prompts:**
```
"Explain the 4-Stage Business Process in VOS"
"How do I conduct a value discovery session?"
"What's the difference between value realization and value delivery?"
"How can I apply VOS principles as a Customer Success Manager?"
```

**Tips:**
- Ask follow-up questions to dive deeper
- Request examples specific to your industry
- Use markdown formatting in responses for better readability

---

### 2. 🎯 KPI Hypothesis Mode

**Purpose:** Generate and quantify value metrics for customer engagements

**Best For:**
- Identifying relevant KPIs for a specific customer scenario
- Quantifying potential business impact
- Creating measurable success criteria
- Framing metrics in executive-friendly language

**How It Works:**
1. Describe your customer's business challenge or goal
2. AI Tutor identifies 3-5 relevant value metrics
3. Provides quantification frameworks (baseline → target → impact)
4. Suggests data sources and validation methods

**Example Prompts:**
```
"Help me identify KPIs for a manufacturing customer looking to reduce downtime"
"What metrics matter for a SaaS company trying to improve customer retention?"
"How do I quantify time savings from process automation?"
```

**Quick Actions:**
- "Help me identify KPIs for cost reduction"
- "What metrics matter for revenue growth?"
- "How do I quantify time savings?"

**Output Format:**
The AI provides structured KPI hypotheses with:
- **Metric Name** (e.g., "Mean Time to Resolution")
- **Baseline** (current state)
- **Target** (desired state)
- **Impact Calculation** (financial or operational)
- **Data Sources** (where to get the numbers)

---

### 3. 💰 ROI Narrative Mode

**Purpose:** Build compelling business cases with clear ROI calculations

**Best For:**
- Structuring executive-ready business cases
- Calculating multi-year ROI with assumptions
- Crafting value stories that resonate with C-suite
- Addressing implementation risks and considerations

**How It Works:**
1. Describe the value opportunity (problem + solution)
2. AI Tutor creates a structured ROI narrative with:
   - **Executive Summary** (1-2 paragraphs)
   - **Financial Model** (costs, benefits, payback period)
   - **Assumptions** (clearly stated and justified)
   - **Risk Mitigation** (implementation considerations)
   - **Success Metrics** (how to measure realization)

**Example Prompts:**
```
"Build a 3-year ROI model for a CRM implementation at a mid-market company"
"Calculate payback period for an AI-powered customer service platform"
"Create an executive summary for a digital transformation initiative"
```

**Quick Actions:**
- "Build a 3-year ROI model"
- "Calculate payback period"
- "Create an executive summary"

**Output Format:**
```markdown
## Executive Summary
[Concise value proposition and financial impact]

## Financial Model
| Year | Investment | Benefits | Net Value | Cumulative ROI |
|------|-----------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Y1   | $X        | $Y       | $Z        | X%             |

## Key Assumptions
1. [Assumption with justification]
2. [Assumption with justification]

## Implementation Considerations
- [Risk + mitigation strategy]

## Success Metrics
- [How to track value realization]
```

---

### 4. 📄 Value Case Mode

**Purpose:** Create reusable value models and document successful patterns

**Best For:**
- Building industry-specific value frameworks
- Documenting successful customer implementations
- Creating templates for repeatable scenarios
- Capturing lessons learned and best practices

**How It Works:**
1. Describe the value scenario you want to document
2. AI Tutor creates a structured value case template with:
   - **Customer Profile** (industry, size, challenges)
   - **Value Delivered** (quantified outcomes)
   - **Methodology Applied** (VOS frameworks used)
   - **Key Insights** (lessons learned)
   - **Reusability Guidance** (when to use this pattern)

**Example Prompts:**
```
"Create a SaaS value case template for reducing customer churn"
"Document a successful implementation of VOS at a Fortune 500 manufacturer"
"Build an industry benchmark for financial services value delivery"
```

**Quick Actions:**
- "Create a SaaS value case template"
- "Document a successful implementation"
- "Build an industry benchmark"

**Output Format:**
```markdown
## Value Case: [Title]

### Customer Profile
- Industry: [X]
- Size: [Y employees / $Z revenue]
- Key Challenges: [List]

### Value Delivered
- Metric 1: [Baseline → Target] = [% improvement]
- Metric 2: [Baseline → Target] = [$ impact]

### VOS Methodology Applied
1. [Framework/Pillar used]
2. [Specific techniques]

### Key Insights
- [Lesson learned #1]
- [Lesson learned #2]

### Reusability Guidance
**When to use:** [Scenario description]
**Adaptations needed:** [Customization points]
```

## Best Practices

### 1. Be Specific
❌ "Help me with value selling"
✅ "I'm meeting with a CFO tomorrow about our analytics platform. Help me build a 3-year ROI model focused on cost reduction from automated reporting."

### 2. Provide Context
Include relevant details:
- Customer industry and size
- Specific business challenges
- Your role in the engagement
- Timeline and constraints

### 3. Iterate and Refine
- Start broad, then narrow down
- Ask follow-up questions
- Request examples or alternatives
- Challenge assumptions to stress-test your thinking

### 4. Use the Right Mode
| Your Goal | Best Mode |
|-----------|-----------|
| Learn VOS concepts | Chat |
| Identify metrics | KPI Hypothesis |
| Build business case | ROI Narrative |
| Document success pattern | Value Case |

### 5. Export and Apply
- Copy generated content to your CRM, presentations, or proposals
- Adapt templates to your specific customer context
- Share value cases with your team for knowledge transfer

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the AI Tutor remember previous conversations?**
A: Yes, within the current session. The conversation history is maintained as long as you stay on the page. Refreshing the page starts a new session.

**Q: Can I use the AI Tutor for customer-facing materials?**
A: Absolutely! The generated content is designed to be professional and ready for adaptation to customer presentations, proposals, and business cases.

**Q: How accurate are the ROI calculations?**
A: The AI provides frameworks and example calculations based on industry benchmarks. Always validate assumptions with your customer's actual data and adjust accordingly.

**Q: Can I switch modes mid-conversation?**
A: Yes! Simply click a different mode tab. The conversation history is preserved, and the AI will adapt its responses to the new mode's focus.

**Q: What if I disagree with the AI's suggestions?**
A: Challenge the AI! Ask "What if..." questions, request alternatives, or provide additional context to refine the output. The AI is a thinking partner, not a replacement for your expertise.
