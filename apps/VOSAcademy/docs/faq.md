# VOS Academy Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Getting Started

### What is VOS Academy?
VOS Academy is a comprehensive learning platform designed to teach Value Operating System (VOS) methodologies. It provides structured learning through 10 foundational pillars, interactive simulations, and AI-powered assessment tools to help professionals master value engineering principles.

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit the VOS Academy login page
2. Click "Sign Up" and enter your email address
3. Complete your profile with your role and experience level
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete the onboarding tour

### What roles are supported?
VOS Academy supports various professional roles including:
- Sales Professionals
- Customer Success Managers
- Marketing Leaders
- Product Managers
- Executive Leadership
- Value Engineering Specialists

## Learning and Certification

### What are the 10 VOS Pillars?
1. **Unified Value Language** - Establish common terminology
2. **Value Data Model** - Structure value quantification
3. **Discovery Excellence** - Identify value opportunities
4. **Business Case Development** - Build compelling justifications
5. **Value Realization** - Execute and measure outcomes
6. **Value Engineering** - Optimize value delivery
7. **Value Communication** - Share insights effectively
8. **Value Culture** - Build organizational capabilities
9. **Value Leadership** - Drive strategic initiatives
10. **Value Innovation** - Create breakthrough solutions

### How does certification work?
Certifications are earned through comprehensive assessment using a 40/30/30 rubric:
- **40% Quiz Performance**: Knowledge assessment
- **30% Simulation Performance**: Application skills
- **30% Role Task Engagement**: Practical engagement metrics

### What are the certification tiers?
- **Bronze**: 80%+ overall score - Solid understanding
- **Silver**: 85%+ overall score - Strong application skills
- **Gold**: 95%+ overall score - Expert-level mastery

### Can I retake assessments?
Yes, you can retake quizzes and simulations to improve your scores. Each attempt provides valuable feedback for improvement.

## Platform Features

### What are simulations?
Simulations are interactive, scenario-based exercises that provide hands-on practice with real-world VOS applications. They include:
- Business Case Development scenarios
- QBR Expansion Modeling exercises
- AI-powered evaluation and feedback

### How does the AI Tutor work?
The AI Tutor is your personal VOS learning assistant that provides:
- ROI Narrative generation
- Value Case building assistance
- KPI Hypothesis validation
- Real-time guidance on VOS concepts
- Personalized learning recommendations

### Can I share my certifications?
Yes, certifications can be shared publicly for professional validation. Each certificate has a unique verification ID that can be used to confirm authenticity.

### How do I track my progress?
Your personalized dashboard shows:
- Pillar completion status
- Certification achievements
- Simulation performance trends
- Learning analytics and insights

## Troubleshooting

### The platform is slow or not loading
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support if issues persist

### I can't access certain features
- Ensure your account has the correct role assigned
- Check if you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support for access issues

### My progress isn't saving
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if data loss occurs

### Assessment scores seem incorrect
- Review the scoring rubric and feedback
- Check if all questions were answered
- Contact support for scoring disputes
