## 3. Radius Tokens

- Root radius token: `--radius: 0.5rem` (8px)
- Tailwind mapping:
  - `rounded-lg` → `var(--radius)`
  - `rounded-md` → `calc(var(--radius) - 2px)`
  - `rounded-sm` → `calc(var(--radius) - 4px)`
- SDUI-specific radii still available:
  - `rounded-sdui-sm` (4px), `rounded-sdui-md` (8px), `rounded-sdui-lg` (12px), `rounded-sdui-xl` (16px)

Use `rounded-lg` for primary cards/buttons to match Aura; `rounded-md` for inputs/pills.
